\section{Overview}
In response to the performance challenge, we adopt many algorithm techniques (\S\ref{sec:algorithm}) and overlap communication with computation (\S\ref{sec:overlap}) as much as possible. We also design an optimized data preprocessing and loading pipeline (\S\ref{sec:data}). By harnessing advanced optimization strategies, we've achieved state-of-the-art MFU using tens of thousands of GPUs. In terms of maintaining performance consistency, we investigated its root causes and identified that occasionally, specific machines lag, resulting in what we've labeled the "straggler issue." To counter this, we introduce a robust monitoring and detection methodology (\S\ref{sec:straggler}). For system-wide bottlenecks, we provide a detailed analysis of the problems faced during large-scale training and suggest our practices and remedies (\S\ref{sec:practice}).
